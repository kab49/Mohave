intro: "Notable for its striking purple-tinted pads, this cactus is a vibrant standout in the arid landscapes of the Desert Southwest. Opuntia gosseliniana is easily recognized by the intense violet hue of its pads, a stress response to high light and drought. While similar to Opuntia santa-rita, it is distinguished by its longer, more slender spines and specific growth habit."
