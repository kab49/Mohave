intro: "A densely spiny cactus of the eastern Mojave, characterized by its upright growth and impressively long, straw-colored spines. Opuntia diploursina is a diploid species that was previously included within the Opuntia erinacea complex. It is distinguished by its incredibly dense spination; the central spines can occasionally reach lengths of up to 6 inches on older parts of the plant, giving it a shaggy, grizzly appearance."
